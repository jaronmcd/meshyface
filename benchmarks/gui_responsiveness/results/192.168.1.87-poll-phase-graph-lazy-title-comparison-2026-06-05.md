## Notes

The graph title optimization preserves detailed native SVG tooltips by creating title nodes lazily on graph edge/node hover or focus. This removes title text from the initial SVG string and avoids parsing hundreds of tooltip nodes during the first graph render.

The remaining high sample totals should not be treated as direct frontend work without the phase details. The saved `pollPerfRuns` show the measured poll work is fast; future benchmark changes should report action time separately from frame/timer wait time.
