## Finding

`renderMap()` already had a map signature fast path, but signal heatmap sync ran before that early return. On unchanged map polls, the dashboard still rebuilt heat buckets and called Leaflet heat-layer `setLatLngs()`. The follow-up caches a heatmap signature that includes mode, visibility, zoom-dependent visual options, desired layer presence, dev knobs, and bucket contents, allowing unchanged polls to skip Leaflet heat layer updates.

One-iteration totals remain noisy because the benchmark waits across refresh boundaries, so max frame delay is the better signal for GUI responsiveness here.
